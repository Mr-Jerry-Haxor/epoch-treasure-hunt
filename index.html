Welcome to TECHScapades

Hey Geeks !!
Are you ready for a quest by putting your techincal knowledge to the test?
Techscapades is the first-ever Technical Treasure Hunt hosted by GitHub Community GITAM.
Eash team would have two hours to follow the clues around campus to find the hidden surprise.
Each team must consist of four members.

Rules

All teams must have four members.
-Each team will be given the starter clue at beginning of the treasure hunt.
-Each clue, once solved, will lead you to the location of the next clue until the final clue leads to the treasure.
-It is mandatory that each team collects all the clues intended to qualify for a win.
-Teams finding the treasure without collecting all the clues will be disqualified.
-The teams will be graded on the following criteria:
-Collection of all the clues and therefore, the treasure -The time it took the team to reach the finishing point
-The breaker - Solve a coding question
